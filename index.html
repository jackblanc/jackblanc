Things I'm a Part Of

Below are some clubs, sports, and activities which I have participated in:

  • Career Advancement and Professional Development Society

    The CAPDS is a club which I am a member of at Bellarmine, and I find it is one of the most useful and productive clubs which I participate in. In the club, we discuss everything from building a resume and LinkedIn profile to internships.

  • Rugby

    I have played Rugby on the Bellarmine College Prep club team since my Freshman year. I love the sport and my teammates, and play in the forward pack as a lock and flanker, if anyone knows what that means.

  • Cross Country

    When I joined Cross Country for Bellarmine as a Freshman, I was in near the worst shape of my life. I ran a 10:19 in the first mile time trial, and got last in my first race. By this most recent season, I ended with a PR of 5:52 in the mile, but more importantly I now love running, which allowed me to appreciate the sport and my teammates more than I ever could have before.

  • Bellarmine Blending Club

    As vice president of the Bellarmine Blending Club, I am responsible for coordination of meetings which proves to be a difficult task due to the fact that there are over 200 members in the club.

  • Kino Club

    I am the secretary of the Bellarmine branch of the national Kino Club association. The Kino Border Initiative is a group which works with people who have been deported, protecting them from the cartels and gangs on their journeys near the border.

  • Coffee Club

    I am secretary of the Bellarmine Coffee Club, which meets weekly to appreciate the awesomeness of coffee. In reality, it is a good break from the high speed life at which all Bellarmine students operate at.
