About me:

My name is Jack Blanc, and this website is an introduction to who I am. I am a junior at Bellarmine College Prep, a rigorous Jesuit college preparatory school in San Jose, California. I am a programmer, with extensive experience in Java. I also have a decent amount of experience in Python and HTML/CSS. In a year and I will be beginning the next chapter of my life as a college student. I hope to pursue a major in Computer Science as well as a minor in Entrepreneurship or Business Administration. These are the two areas which interest me most as of now because I believe there is much potential for success in the combination of the technology and business worlds. In my current life as a high school student, I run cross country and play rugby, as well as leading and participating in various clubs at school.
I am a motivated student with vision and a determination to succeed in an area that I am passionate about.
