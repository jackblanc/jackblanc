A Rugby Player

Here I talk about my experience playing Rugby, an awesome sport!

Rugby

Also in my freshman year, I joined Bellarmine's club rugby team. An old friend of my grandmother is the coach, so he reccomended that I play rugby, and I decided to take his advice. I had never played any rugby before in my life. Thankfully, about half the team had never played before as well, so I wasn't alone in my lack of any experience. I learned to play flanker and lock, and ended up actually enjoying the sport. I became friends with many people on the team, so that made it even more fun to play with them. The game itself is very mentally challenging. It takes a lot of determination and strength, both mentally and physically, which has given me skills I have applied to all walks of my life.
